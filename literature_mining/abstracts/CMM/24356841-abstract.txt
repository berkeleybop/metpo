Title: Draft Genome Sequence of the Methanotrophic Gammaproteobacterium Methyloglobulus morosus DSM 22980 Strain KoM1.

Authors: Poehlein A, Deutzmann JS, Daniel R, Simeonova DD.

Journal:  (2013)

DOI: 10.1128/genomea.01078-13
PMID: 24356841
PMCID: PMC3868865

Abstract:
Here, we report the draft genome sequence of the methanotrophic gammaproteobacterium Methyloglobulus morosus DSM 22980 strain KoM1, which is proposed to be the type species for the novel genus Methyloglobulus. The genome (4.143Â Mb) consists of a single circular chromosome and harbors genes for 2-aminoethylphosphonate (ciliatine) biosynthesis.
